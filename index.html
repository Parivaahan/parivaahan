About Parivaahan

A digital platform bringing transport department services to your fingertips.

The Parivaahan initiative aims to simplify and digitize all vehicle and transport related services provided by the government. Our mission is to make these services accessible, transparent and hassle-free for citizens.

Get Started with Parivaahan

Thank you for your interest in Parivaahan services!

To begin, please select a service, provide all necessary details, and receive your documents.

  • **Select Service:** Choose the specific vehicle or transport service you need.
  • **Enter Details:** Provide all required information and upload any necessary documents.
  • **Receive Service:** Get your updated registration, license, or other documents digitally or physically.
