Instructions for Application Submission
Following are the stages in Application Submission in Issuing Driving Licence in the following order:
  1. 1. Fill Applicant Details
  2. 2. Upload Documents
  3. 3. Upload Photo and Signature if required (applicable for only some states)
  4. 4. DL Test Slot Booking (applicable for only some states)
  5. 5. Payment of Fee
  6. 6. Verify the Pay Status
  7. 7. Print the receipt

Citizens without having Aadhaar may please approach RTO office with valid documents for verification and Application Submission.
